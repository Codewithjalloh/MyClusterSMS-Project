About Cluster SMS

Ultimate SMS is a Bulk SMS Marketing app that is powerful, adaptable, and easy to use. It also serves as an all-in-one SMS marketing solution. It's simple to implement and utilise.

Our technical support staff will contact you as soon as possible. With cluster sms, we've already integrated more than 50 sms gateway APIs. You can pick one of these, or you can use cluster sms to add your gateway.

Our Features

Management of clients: Clients are the most crucial individuals in any business. Ultimate SMS is a programme that allows you to keep a closer eye on your customers in a more efficient and straightforward manner.

SMS Templates with Personalization: Create bespoke SMS templates that are absolutely safe and will help you save time. Simply design your template, save it in the system, and then utilise it as needed.

Management of Sender IDs: With dynamic SMS sender ID, you can make a basic text message into a strong branding tool by displaying your company name as the sender. For each client, you may ban or unblock Sender ID.

FEATURES

Send SMS at a Predetermined Time

When you use Scheduled Message, you may reach out to yourself or a group of individuals at the same time. Simply choose your client groups or upload csv files, then choose your time. The message will be delivered on schedule..

Clean SMS Reports

Ultimate SMS provides you with a clean, basic, and visually appealing SMS history report. Your sms history will be shown in the same way as your mobile inbox.

Role Management with No Limits

Role management aids authorisation by allowing you to designate which resources users in your application are permitted to access. By assigning users to roles such as manager, sales, member, and so on, role management allows you to handle groups of users as a unit..

Popular SMS Gateways Integrated

More than 20 of the most popular SMS gateways are included in Ultimate SMS. Using these gateways, you may send SMS to your consumers or clients. You may also add your own non-integrated gateway to Ultimate SMS.

Support for several languages

Ultimate SMS may be translated into any language. Simply enter your translated nation name, upload your country flag, and then convert your English value to your own. That does not necessitate any programming skills.

Appearance

Appearance Ultimate SMS is simple to modify, and anybody may make changes to it. You may make it your own without any hassle if you want to.
